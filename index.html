Fibonacci Painter Processing and JS painting app based around the Fibonacci sequence

fibonacci-static

Click and drag mouse to draw.

Your browser does not support the canvas tag.

draw-curve

Click to start curve. Click again to stop current curve and start new one.

Your browser does not support the canvas tag.

spiral-looper

Click and hold to draw spiral. Release mouse to stop.

fibonacci-square

Click and hold to start drawing. Release mouse to stop.
